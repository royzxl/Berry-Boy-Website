Our Story

Hey everyone ! This is Kent and Andrew from Berry Boy . . .

We started Berry Boy in March of 2024 in hopes of changing Hurstville from the ends where no one stays out past 8pm into a place that where families, kids, adults, couples, really anyone, could hang out. One year later, we can proudly say that with the help of our selfless team, we’ve built a community of all you acai addicts. We’re here to keep pushing the boundaries of tasty, and healthier dessert options to one day hopefully create a livelier Sydney. Thanks for supporting the journey and if your new here, follow along with our little fun project :D
